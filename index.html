Heroes

Deadpool

Antihero

The character, known for his talkative nature, has been nicknamed the "Merc with a Mouth". Deadpool has sometimes been portrayed to have a strong sense of core morality.

Batman

Gotham Hero

Batman is the superhero protector of Gotham City, a tortured, brooding vigilante dressed as a sort of human bat who fights against evil and strikes fear into the hearts of criminals everywhere. In his public identity he is Bruce Wayne, billionaire industrialist and notorious playboy.

Flash

Scarlet Speedster

The Flash. ... He is the Flash. After a particle accelerator causes a freak storm, CSI Investigator Barry Allen is struck by lightning and falls into a coma. Months later he awakens with the power of super speed, granting him the ability to move through Central City like an unseen guardian angel.

Spider-Man

Friendly Neighborhood

The american teenager Peter Parker, a poor sickly orphan, is bitten by a radioactive spider. As a result of the bite, he gains superhuman strength, speed, and agility along with the ability to cling to walls.
